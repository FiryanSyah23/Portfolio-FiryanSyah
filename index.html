Digital Twins

Digital Twins is a solar panel monitoring tool that can read current and voltage in real time, simulate sunlight intensity using spotlights as artificial light sources, and automatically control potentiometers via Arduino Mega-based servo motors. This system also allows users to change the configuration of solar panels between series and parallel circuits so that they can directly analyze the effect of varying operating conditions on the electrical output characteristics of solar panels, making it an interactive and applicable medium for learning and experimentation.
